Objective

The objective of this project is to create an immersive and impactful interactive fiction experience using Twine, centered around the theme of stalking. "Watch" aims to raise awareness about the seriousness of stalking while providing players with a compelling and thought-provoking narrative experience.

Project Description

"Watch" is an interactive fiction experience that takes the player on a captivating journey through the day. The narrative explores themes of identity, memory, and perception, presenting the player with choices that influence the outcome of the story. Through branching paths and interactive elements, the project aims to demonstrate the potential of interactive storytelling in engaging and captivating users.

Key Features:

  • Immersive Storytelling: I crafted a gripping and suspenseful narrative that keeps players engaged from beginning to end.
  • Choice-Based Gameplay: the game offers players meaningful choices that impact the outcome of the story, allowing for multiple branching paths and endings.
  • Realistic Characterization: I aimed to develop complex and relatable characters, including the protagonist, the stalker, and supporting characters to enhance immersion and emotional investment.
  • Psychological Depth: Explore the psychological effects of stalking on the protagonist's mental health and well-being, highlighting the trauma and resilience inherent in such experiences.
  • Awareness and Advocacy: Use the interactive medium to raise awareness about stalking and its impact on individuals and communities.

Target Audience

The target audience for "Watch" includes individuals interested in interactive storytelling, advocates for social justice and awareness, and those seeking to understand the realities of stalking and its impact on victims.

Time Line

Week 1: Concept development. Writing and scripting the branching narrative paths in Twine

Week 2: Testing and refinement based on user feedback, ensuring sensitivity to the subject matter. Final polishing, including visual design, sound effects, and multimedia integration.

Deliverables

  • Fully functional Twine project showcasing the interactive fiction experience.
  • Documentation outlining design decisions, user testing results, and reflections on the development process.
  • Resources and support materials for individuals affected by stalking, included within the project or provided separately.
